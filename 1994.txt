The owners of Taj India, Manjeet and Kamal who had moved from California, opened Taj India at 2226 Highland Avenue in the Highland Plaza shopping center. At this time, the had a $5.95 lunch buffet that made Indian food accessible for many of the locals. This introduced many Birmingham residents to popular Indian dishes like naan, curries, etc. This is what helped to put Taj India on the map being the city's first Indian restaurant.
