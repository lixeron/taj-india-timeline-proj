With the outbreak of the COVID-19 virus spreading in Birmingham, Taj India posted a detailed update about their new safety regulations on their Facebook. They explain the use of extra cleaning and sanitizing, the additions of hand-sanitizer stations, and the changes of how customers would be served in the restaraunt. Along with this, they also stated that tumeric is an antioxidant and lemon "finger bowls" being a mild disinfectant both of which are used in most of their dishes. This shows they are taking actions for the virus and are providing possible ways that their Indian cuisine can help with both health and cleanliness.
